If you encounter a bug or have a feature request, please [create a new issue](https://github.com/tanizya/acubeat-support/issues/new).

## Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

### Overview

AcuBeat ("the App") is a rhythm accuracy game. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

### Data Collection

AcuBeat does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require account registration or login.

### Data Stored on Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server:

- Game scores and play history
- User preferences (color theme, latency offset, audio offset, metronome mode)

This data can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the App.

### Third-Party Services

- **Game Center (Apple)**: If you choose to use Game Center, your scores are submitted to Apple's Game Center service. This is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).
- **In-App Purchases (Apple)**: Purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. No payment information is accessed or stored by the App.
- **Advertising**: The App may display ads provided by Apple. Ad interactions are governed by Apple's advertising policies.

### Children's Privacy

AcuBeat does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

### Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please [open an issue](https://github.com/tanizya/acubeat-support/issues/new).
